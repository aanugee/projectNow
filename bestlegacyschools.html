At Best Legacy, the Playgroup Curriculum and the Nursery Curriculum launches the Children into a world of Exploration and builds on their exploration. Early Experiences in the first few years of life are critical for acquisition of skills as maximum brain development happens during this period.We foster children's development by learning and practising through a scientific approach. Best Legacy fosters social, aesthetic and motor skills in young children.As they progress into the nursery age group,we also ensure the following:
  • Learning by exploration
  • Building Communication Skills
  • Encouraging positive peer interaction
  • Theme based concept time
  • Language Readiness
  • Opportunities for fine and gross motor development
  • Promoting cognitive development
